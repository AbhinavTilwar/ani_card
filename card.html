Tiger

They are the largest naturally occurring species of cat

  • Scientific Name: Panthera tigris
  • Average Length: 11 feet
  • Average Lifespan: 19 to 20 years
  • Habitat: mangrove swamps to grasslands

A Tiger is a powerful carnivore, a hunter with sharp teeth, strong jaws, and a very agile body. Tigers are an endangered species because their habitat is disappearing. The closest relative to the tiger is a lion.
